Our Story

Built by coaches who got tired of guessing.

How It Started

2019. A garage, a whiteboard, and eleven athletes.

IronCore didn't start as a gym — it started as a spreadsheet. Our founder, a former competitive powerlifter, was frustrated watching people train hard for years without ever tracking whether it worked.

So he built a system: every session logged, every lift measured, every plan adjusted weekly based on real data — not guesswork. Eleven athletes trained in that garage. Within a year, all eleven hit personal records they'd chased for years.

That system is still the core of how we coach today — we've just added better equipment.
