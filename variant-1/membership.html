No Guesswork

Every plan comes with a tracked program — not just gym access.

A lot of gyms sell you a door code and call it a membership. At IronCore, even our Basic plan includes a monthly check-in with a coach, because access without direction rarely turns into progress.

The higher tiers simply add more direct coaching time — the tracking and accountability system is built into everything we offer.

Common Questions

Membership FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at the start of any billing cycle with no penalty.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee on any plan. Your first session is always free, regardless of the tier you choose.

What if I need to pause my membership?

Memberships can be paused for up to 60 days per year for travel, injury, or exams — just notify us in advance.
